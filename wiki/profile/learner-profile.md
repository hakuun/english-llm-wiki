# Learner Profile

## Background

- Native language: Chinese.
- Current English vocabulary: about 1,000 basic words.
- Goal: reach general CEFR B2 in about one year.
- Daily study time: about 30 minutes.

## Current learning priorities

1. Reading.
2. Listening.
3. Vocabulary.
4. Grammar.
5. Writing.
6. Speaking as a lighter supporting skill.

## Language preference

Use Chinese for explanations, planning, and diagnosis by default. Keep English examples, target sentences, vocabulary items, collocations, and corrected writing in English.

As the learner approaches B1 and above, gradually increase simple English explanations when helpful.

## Speaking policy

Speaking is not the main short-term focus, but should not be removed. Use low-pressure speaking-related tasks:

- reading aloud,
- shadowing,
- short retelling,
- spoken-style answers.
