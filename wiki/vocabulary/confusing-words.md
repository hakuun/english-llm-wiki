## to me vs for me

**Core difference:**

| | to me | for me |
|---|---|---|
| 含义 | 动作的**方向/接收者** | 动作的**受益者/替我做** |
| 中文 | "给……"、"向……" | "为……"、"替……" |

**Rule of thumb：**

> 动词在"传递东西/信息"给一个人 → 用 **to**
> 动词在"替那个人做/为那个人好" → 用 **for**

**Common verbs that take to me（传递类动词）：**

| verb + to me | 例句 |
|---|---|
| give ... to me | Give the book to me. 把书给我。 |
| tell ... to me | Tell the story to me. 把故事讲给我听。 |
| send ... to me | Send the email to me. 把邮件发给我。 |
| show ... to me | Show the photo to me. 把照片给我看。 |
| explain ... to me | Explain the rule to me. 把规则解释给我听。 |
| recommend ... to me | Recommend a book to me. 向我推荐一本书。 |
| say ... to me | What did he say to you? 他对你说了什么？ |

**Common verbs that take for me（受益/代替类动词）：**

| verb + for me | 例句 |
|---|---|
| buy ... for me | I bought this book for you. 我买了这本书给你。 |
| cook ... for me | She cooked dinner for me. 她为我做了晚饭。 |
| do ... for me | Can you do this for me? 你能替我做这个吗？ |
| sign ... for me | Please sign this for me. 请替我签一下。 |
| open ... for me | He opened the door for me. 他替我开了门。 |

**对比例句（同一个动词，换介词意思就变）：**

| 用 to（方向） | 用 for（受益/代替） |
|---|---|
| Send the book **to** me. 书寄给我。 | Send the book **for** me. 替我把书寄了（我没法自己寄）。 |
| Read the story **to** me. 把故事读给我听。 | Read the story **for** me. 替我把这个故事读了（比如帮我看完）。 |
| Recommend a book **to** me. 向我推荐一本书。 | Recommend a book **for** me. 替我去推荐一本书（给别人）。 |

**Common learner mistake:**

```text
❌ My wife recommends a book for me.   （意思变成：妻子替我去推荐书给别人）
✅ My wife recommends a book to me.    （妻子向我推荐一本书）
```

**Practice (fill in to or for):**

1. Can you explain this word ___ me?
2. My mom cooked a big dinner ___ me.
3. He sent a message ___ me yesterday.
4. I bought this gift ___ you.
5. She recommended this podcast ___ me.

Answers: 1. to  2. for  3. to  4. for  5. to
