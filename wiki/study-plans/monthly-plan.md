# Monthly Plan

This page records the current month-level study direction. It should be updated during monthly assessment, or earlier only when the learner's evidence clearly changes the plan.

## 2026-05 current plan

### Current stage

- Working level: A2 to early B1 for familiar writing and guided study.
- Main constraint: about 30 minutes per day.
- Main method: review-first guided daily pages, not blank free writing.
- Input baseline: A2, moving toward A2-B1 when recent daily evidence shows the task is suitable.

### Month focus

1. Build a stable daily study loop.
2. Use short A2 or A2-B1 reading/listening input with simple comprehension checks.
3. Review a small active vocabulary set instead of collecting many new words.
4. Repair recurring sentence errors through guided practice.
5. Collect enough reading, listening, vocabulary, grammar, and writing evidence for the first monthly checkpoint.

### Skill balance

- Reading/listening input: keep it short and comprehensible.
- Vocabulary: 3-6 active words or chunks per daily page.
- Grammar/errors: 1-2 focus points per daily page.
- Output: guided sentences first; optional free output only as a challenge.
- Speaking support: light reading aloud or shadowing when useful.

### Current priority evidence to collect

- Can the learner understand short A2 texts without word-by-word translation?
- Can the learner handle short listen-read tasks with a transcript or self-reading?
- Can the learner use active words such as `improve`, `spend`, and `materials` in guided sentences?
- Are errors with plural nouns, place prepositions, and `There are` / `They are` decreasing?
