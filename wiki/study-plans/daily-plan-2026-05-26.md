昨天主要有 2 个问题：

### 问题 1：阅读细节错误

原文：

```text
In the morning, I read five English sentences.
In the evening, I write three sentences in my notebook.
```

昨天你把晚上写几句话答成了 5 个。正确是：

```text
He / She writes three sentences in the evening.
```

注意区分：

- five sentences = 早上读 5 个句子
- three sentences = 晚上写 3 个句子

### 问题 2：`There are` 和 `They are` 混淆

昨天你写：

```text
There are very happy.
```

这个句子不自然。正确应是：

```text
They are very happy.
```

记住：

```text
They are very happy. = 他们很开心。
There are three sentences. = 有三个句子。
```

## 5. 今日语法 / 句型：5 分钟

今天练习“从过去持续到现在”的表达。

```text
I have been working as a developer since 2020.
I have worked as a front-end developer for five years.
```

记住两个结构：

```text
I have been + doing + since + 时间点
I have worked as + 职业 + for + 时间长度
```

中文对比：

- since 2020 = 从 2020 年开始到现在
- for five years = 持续五年
