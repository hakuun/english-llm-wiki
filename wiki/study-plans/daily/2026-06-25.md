## 3. Warm-up（5 分钟）

### 🟡 like-more-than vs be-er-than — 边界澄清

> 昨天你写了 `I like science fiction is more than fantasy fiction.`
>
> 你把比较句 be 动词规则学进去了（这是好事！），但推得太远了——**句子已经有动词，就不再加 is。**

```
🔑 两种"比"：

① A is + 比较级 + than B        →  句子缺动词，用 is 补上
   Watermelon is sweeter than strawberry. ✅
   Mango is more delicious than apple. ✅

② like A more than B             →  句子已有动词 like，不需要 is
   I like science fiction more than fantasy fiction. ✅
   She likes mango more than banana. ✅

💡 判断口诀：看一眼——句子里有动词吗？
   有动词（like, prefer, want）→ 不加 is
   没动词                   → 加 is
```

**改错（1 题）：**

```
下面哪个句子是对的？

A. I like watermelon more than strawberry.
B. I like watermelon is more than strawberry.

你的答案：[A]
```
