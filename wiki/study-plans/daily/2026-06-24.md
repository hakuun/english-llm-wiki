### 🔄 by + doing 轻提醒（1 题改错）

> 昨天你写了 `by hard learn every day`，应该用 **by studying hard**。
>
> **规则（5 秒扫一眼）**：
> ```
> by 后面动词加 -ing → by doing something
> 
> ✅ by studying hard     ✅ by reading every day
> ✅ by listening          ✅ by practicing
> ❌ by hard learn         ❌ by work hard
> ```

**找错并改正：**

```

错误：_______by read__________________________________________
改正：______She improved her English by reading English books every night.___________________________________________
```

### 🔑 比较级 -er 规则（1 分钟知识点）

> 昨天你写了 `Mango is more sweet than banana.`
>
> 比较句 be 动词你有 ✅！但比较级的形式需要注意——**短形容词不跟 more，要加 -er**。

```
🔑 比较级两条路：

短形容词（1 个音节）→ 词尾加 -er（不用 more）
  sweet  → sweeter      big    → bigger
  small  → smaller       hot   → hotter
  cheap  → cheaper       tall  → taller

长形容词（2+ 个音节）→ 前面加 more
  expensive → more expensive
  delicious → more delicious
  difficult → more difficult

💡 结尾单辅音前是短元音 → 双写辅音字母再加 -er
   big → bigger    hot → hotter    thin → thinner
```

**试一个：**

```

2. A plane is ______more expensive_________ (expensive) than a bus.

| 问题 | 说明 | 修正 |
