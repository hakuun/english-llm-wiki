### Part C — Second listen: 看着 transcript 听（5 分钟）

**Transcript（精简版 — 只保留核心对话）**

**Neil**: Hi Becca. How are you?

**Becca**: I'm really well. I've recently been to the beach. I went to visit my parents, and they live quite close to the beach.

**Neil**: When you have a beach day, do you like to **sunbathe** — lying on the beach doing nothing — or do you prefer to do something active, like swimming?

**Becca**: I do love to sunbathe. I'll sunbathe for a little bit and then I'll **go for a swim** to **cool down**. I prefer more relaxing activities. How about you?

**Neil**: I prefer to do something active — swimming, beach football, beach cricket. And if I am sunbathing, I like to read a book, but it's a bit uncomfortable.

**Becca**: You mentioned playing sports on the beach. I imagine it can be quite difficult to play sports on a **pebbly beach**. Pebbles are small stones, and very different to **sandy beaches**. Sand is quite soft.

**Neil**: Yes, lots of beaches in the UK are pebble beaches. And yes, it can really hurt your feet.

**Becca**: How about food? Do you take a **picnic** to the beach?

**Becca**: I really love a pebble beach because it's easier to have a picnic on a pebble beach. Every time I sit on a sandy beach with a lovely picnic, it always gets ruined by all of the sand.

**Neil**: Exactly. You end up with a kind of **crunchy** sandwich.

**Becca**: Sandy sandwiches — not tasty!

**Neil**: Have you got a memory of a favourite beach you've been to?

**Becca**: Yes — when I was in Thailand last February. I just remember seeing the **sunset** at the beach, and I'd never seen the Sun so red before. It was incredible. Just beautiful. **Stunning**.

## 5. Vocabulary（5 分钟）

### 🔄 到期词快速回顾（5 词）

> 下面这些词已经超过 review 日期，今天快速过一遍。

**1. accident**（A2 ⭐）— 事故，意外

```
短语：car accident / by accident（不小心、意外地）

填空：I haven't played tennis since my _________ (accident).
你的答案：______accident_________
```

**2. opportunity**（B1 ⭐）— 机会

```
= chance，但更正式
搭配：look for opportunities / have an opportunity to do

填空：A good language learner always looks for _________ (opportunity) to talk with other students.
你的答案：_______opportunities________
```

**3. imagine**（B1 ⭐）— 想象

```
搭配：imagine yourself doing / I can't imagine...
例句：Imagine yourself speaking English every day.

用 imagine 写一句（想象你在一个很美的海滩上）：
你的答案：______________________________________________________________
```

**4. communicate**（B1 ⭐）— 交流

```
搭配：communicate with
例句：People use language to communicate with other people.

填空：I use English to _________ (communicate) with my colleagues.
你的答案：______communicate_________
```

**5. prefer**（A1）— 更喜欢

```
搭配：prefer A to B / prefer doing / prefer to do
例句：I prefer reading to watching TV.

用 prefer 写一句（你在海滩上 prefer 做什么？）：
你的答案：______________________________________________________________
```
