## 6. Grammar / sentence pattern

今天只认识最基础的 be 动词结构。

```text
I am ...
It is ...
Some words are ...
```

Examples:

- I am a beginner.
- English is not easy for me.
- Some words are difficult.

Remember:

```text
I am + 身份/状态
It is + 形容词
Some words are + 形容词
```

### Imitation

1. I am a **__**.
2. It is **__**.
3. Some words are **__**.

Learner answers:

1. I am a developer.
2. She is my wife.
3. There are very happy.

Key correction:

- `There are very happy.` should be `They are very happy.` if the meaning is “他们很开心”。
