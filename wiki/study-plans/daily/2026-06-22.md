**Q4 解析**：

> - A. Elizabeth School of English 的 General English 需要至少 2 个学生才能开课。
> - B. Three Schools English 的 Intensive course 每周只上 4 天课。

#### 7. Option B — 比较两所学校

**句 1**：比价格

```
你的句子：Three Schools English more expensive than Elizabeth School of English.
```

- ❌ **缺动词！** 比较句需要 be 动词：Three Schools English **IS** more expensive than...
- 📝 这就是你 stuck points 里说的"找不到动词"——比较句里 **is/are 就是动词**。中文说"A 比 B 贵"，没有"是"字，所以英文写比较句特别容易漏 be 动词。

✅ 修正：**Three Schools English is more expensive than Elizabeth School of English.**

**句 2**：比灵活度

```
你的句子：You should choose your own School.
```

- ⚠️ `School` → `school`（普通名词不用大写）
- 句子本身语法没问题，但和"比较灵活度"的要求不太对应。如果能点出 Elizabeth School 更灵活就更好，比如：`Elizabeth School is more flexible because you can choose your own modules.` 不过作为仿写练习，你的句子是通的。

**句 3**：你的选择

```
你的句子：I want to choose Elizabeth School of English, Because they teachers are all qualified and experienced.
```

- ⚠️ `Because` → `because`（逗号后面用小写）
- ❌ `they teachers` → **their** teachers（"他们的老师"= their，不是 they）

> **they vs their 快速对比**：
> - **they** = 他们（主语，做动作的人）：**They** are teachers.
> - **their** = 他们的（物主代词，后面跟名词）：**their** teachers / **their** school / **their** books
> - 记忆：中文"他们的老师"→ 英文"their teachers"，不是"they teachers"

✅ 修正：**I want to choose Elizabeth School of English because their teachers are all qualified and experienced.**

### Stuck points 反馈
