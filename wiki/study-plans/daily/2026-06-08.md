#### 复述（逐句批改）

**Q1** — `Neil likes reading long fiction stroy,`

```text
⚠️ Neil likes reading long fiction stroy.
✅ Neil likes reading long novels.
```

- `stroy` → `stories`（拼写），但 `long fiction stories` 啰嗦 → 直接说 `long novels`
- 内容准确 👍

**Q2** — `Opposite, Becca likes short poems,`

```text
⚠️ Opposite, Becca likes short poems.
✅ In contrast, Becca likes short poems.
```

- `Opposite` 作连接词不自然 → `In contrast` 或 `On the other hand`
- 句子结构、复数都正确 👍

**Q3** — `But They all like reading in quiet envmotion,`

```text
❌ But They all like reading in quiet envmotion,
✅ But they both like reading in a quiet environment.
```

| 问题 | 你的写法 | 正确 |
|------|---------|------|
| 两人用 both | They all | they both |
| 拼写（×2） | envmotion | environment |
| 缺冠词 a | quiet environment | a quiet environment |

**Q4** — `Because quiet envmotion let them relaxt`

```text
❌ Because quiet envmotion let them relaxt.
✅ Because a quiet environment makes them feel relaxed.
```

| 问题 | 你的写法 | 正确 |
|------|---------|------|
| 拼写 | envmotion, relaxt | environment, relaxed |
| 三单 -s | let（主语 a quiet environment 是三单） | makes |
| 结构 | let them relaxed（中文直译"让他们放松"） | makes them feel relaxed |

**"让某人感到……"的英语结构：**
```text
❌ let someone + adj     （中文直译，英语不这么说）
✅ make someone feel + adj
✅ help someone relax
```

**Q5** — `and Neil also likes reading autobiography, he reads serious when he reads musican autobiography`

```text
❌ and Neil also likes reading autobiography, he reads serious when he reads musican autobiography
✅ Neil also likes reading autobiographies. He reads seriously when he reads musicians' autobiographies.
```

| 问题 | 你的写法 | 正确 |
|------|---------|------|
| 泛指用复数 | autobiography | autobiographies |
| run-on sentence | `, he reads...` | `. He reads...` |
| 形容词→副词 | reads serious | reads seriously |
| 拼写 | musican | musician |
| 缺所有格 | musican autobiography | musicians' autobiographies |
