## 4. Grammar input — 现在完成时 + for / since（8 分钟）

### 先看 4 个例句

来自 Oxford 语法 + 学过的材料：

```
1. She has lived here for over 30 years.        （她在这里住了 30 多年了）
2. I haven't played tennis since my accident.    （自从受伤后我就没打过网球）
3. I have worked as a developer for five years.  （我做了 5 年开发）
4. He has known Maya since he was twelve.        （他从 12 岁起就认识 Maya）
```

### 核心规则（3 个要点，用中文）

**规则 1**：`for` + **一段时间**（多久）

> for four years / for two days / for a week / for a long time

```
✅ I have lived here for three years.
❌ I have lived here since three years.  ← 错！since 后面不能接"一段时间"
```

**规则 2**：`since` + **一个时间点**（从什么时候开始）

> since 2020 / since Monday / since last month / since I was a child / since I started working

```
✅ I have lived here since 2023.
✅ I have known him since we were in school.
❌ I have known him for 2019.  ← 错！for 后面不能接"年份"
```

**简单记法**：
```
for  = 多久（时间段）
since = 从……开始（时间点）

for 5 years ✅     since 5 years ❌
since 2021 ✅      for 2021 ❌
```

**规则 3**：问"多久"用 `How long...？` + 现在完成时

```
问：How long have you lived here?   （你在这里住了多久？）
答：For three years. / Since 2023.
```

### ⚠️ 关键区分：现在完成时 vs 一般过去时（复习 06-09 的内容）

```
现在完成时：I have worked here for 5 years.     （现在还在这里工作）
一般过去时：I worked there for 3 years.          （已经不在那里工作了，结束了）
```

**同样的 for，不同的时态，不同的意思！**

| | 现在完成时 + for | 一般过去时 + for |
|---|---|---|
| 意思 | 还在做，还在持续 | 已经结束 |
| 例子 | I have lived here **for** 3 years.（现在还住这里） | I lived in Beijing **for** 2 years.（已经不住北京了） |
