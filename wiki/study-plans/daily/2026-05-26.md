## 7. Grammar / sentence pattern

今天练习“从过去持续到现在”的表达。

```text
I have been working as a developer since 2020.
I have worked as a front-end developer for five years.
```

Remember:

```text
I have been + doing + since + 时间点
I have worked as + 职业 + for + 时间长度
```

中文对比：

- since 2020 = 从 2020 年开始到现在
- for five years = 持续五年

### Imitation

1. I have been working as a developer since **2020**.
2. I have worked as a front-end developer for **five years**.
3. I have been learning English for **one day / one month / one year**.

Learner answers:

1. I have been learning English since 2026.
2. I have lived ChangSha for four years.
3. I have been marrying for one year.

Key corrections:

1. `I have been learning English since 2026.` is grammatically possible if the starting year is 2026.
2. `I have lived in Changsha for four years.`
3. `I have been married for one year.`

## 8. Small output

Write 4 English sentences.

Remember:

- 句首大写。
- `I` 永远大写。
- 表示“他们是……”用 `They are ...`；表示“有……”用 `There are ...`。

Model:

1. I am a front-end developer.
2. I have been working since 2020.
3. I want to improve my English within one year.
4. There are many English documents at work.

### Learner output

1. I am a handsome.
2. I have been useing AI since 2024.
3. I want to keep healthy within one year.
4. There are a lot of food in table.

Key corrections:

1. `I am handsome.` / `I am a handsome man.`
2. `I have been using AI since 2024.`
3. `I want to stay healthy within one year.` is understandable, but `I want to become healthier within one year.` is more natural.
4. `There is a lot of food on the table.`
