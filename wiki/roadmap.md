# Roadmap

Goal: help a Chinese native speaker with about 1,000 basic English words reach general CEFR B2 in about one year, using about 30 minutes of study per day.

## Main priorities

1. Build high-frequency vocabulary and collocations.
2. Develop stable A2-B1 reading and listening comprehension first.
3. Use grammar as a tool for understanding and self-correction.
4. Add small but consistent output practice through sentences, summaries, short answers, and paragraph writing.
5. Gradually move from comprehensible learning materials to authentic B2-level materials.

## Stage overview

- Months 1-3: A2 to early B1 foundation.
- Months 4-8: B1 consolidation and transition toward B2.
- Months 9-12: B2-level input, writing, summarizing, and self-expression.

## Operating principle

The wiki should not become a storage dump. Every page should support better study decisions, better source use, better review, or better correction of recurring problems.
