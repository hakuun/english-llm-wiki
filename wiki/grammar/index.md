## Current focus

1. Possessives: `my/your/his/her/its/our/their`, possessive pronouns, possessive `'s`.
2. Quantifiers: `some/any`, `much/many`, `a few/a little`, `(not) enough`.
3. Present perfect simple: `have/has + past participle`.
4. Present perfect vs past simple.
5. Past perfect for reading and story order.

For now, learn grammar through short personal sentences, not long rule memorization.
