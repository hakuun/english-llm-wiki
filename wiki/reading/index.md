## Current reading strategy

Use short A1-A2/B1 texts as daily input. For each text, extract only:

1. 5-8 useful chunks,
2. 2-3 sentence patterns worth imitating,
3. 1 short output task.

Do not over-summarize easy texts; turn them into usable speaking/writing patterns.
