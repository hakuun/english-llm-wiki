## Why it is useful

水果是日常高频话题，对话简短自然，适合 A1-A2 阶段做听力解码练习。核心词汇（sour, ripe, juicy, dried fruit）在描述食物时非常实用。最后一段的 recapping 示范了如何用简单英语解释词义——这是 B1 口语考试的重要能力。

## Key vocabulary

| Word | CEFR | Definition (from transcript) |
|------|------|------|
| sour | B1 | having a sharp or acidic taste |
| ripe | B1 | ready to eat, usually juicy |
| juicy | B1 | full of juice or liquid |
| dried fruit | A2 | fruit that has had most of its water removed |
| citrus fruit | B1 | fruit like oranges, lemons, limes |
| crunchy | B1 | making a sharp sound when you bite (apple) |

## Key chunks

- `How are you doing today?` — 比 "How are you?" 更口语化
- `I try to eat fruit every day.` — `try to + verb` 表达习惯
- `Do you prefer sweet fruit or sour fruit?` — `prefer A or B` 选择问句
- `If you could only eat one fruit for the rest of your life, which would you choose?` — 虚拟条件问句（有趣的口语练习模板）
- `It feels quite strange to not eat a banana every day.` — `It feels + adj + to + verb` 表达感受
- `there's lots of things you can do with bananas` — `there's lots of things you can do with + noun` 万能口语模板

## Grammar points

- **形容词描述食物口感**：sour, sweet, juicy, crunchy, ripe — 都是系动词后接形容词（`it is sour`, `it tastes sweet`）
- **would + choose**：第二条件句的口语用法（`If you could only eat one fruit ... which would you choose?`）

## Shadowing 推荐段落

Neil 解释 ripe 的定义（~15 秒，语速慢，句子短）：

> It means that it's ready to eat. It's at its sweetest usually, probably quite juicy. If fruit is juicy, it means juice comes from it. And juice is a liquid — watery. And it's... If it's ripe, it's just before it goes bad or goes off.

## Retelling 提示

用 3-5 句英文回答：
- What fruits do Becca and Neil like?
- What does "ripe" mean?
- Do you prefer sweet fruit or sour fruit? Why?
