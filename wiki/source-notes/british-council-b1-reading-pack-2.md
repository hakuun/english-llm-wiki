## Key chunks by category

### Workplace and formal email（职场邮件 + 面试）

- `I would truly appreciate your advice on this.` — 礼貌请求
- `It shouldn't take longer than two hours of your time.` — 预估时间
- `We would be happy to clear it with Patricia, your department head.` — 协调权限
- `Thank you for your application for the position of ...` — 面试邀请开场
- `You will have the opportunity to find out more about ...` — 正式措辞
- `Please bring your CV and references to the interview.` — 面试准备
- `I look forward to meeting you.` — 正式结尾

### Academic and study（学业 + 课程）

- `Well done, Javier, on all your hard work this term.` — 老师评语
- `The area you need to work on the most is ...` — 指出弱点
- `I recommend more practice at home.` — 学习建议
- `Choose the modules to suit your needs.` — 个性化选择
- `available with a minimum of two students` — 条件表达
- `make notes during every class` / `use a dictionary` / `do extra practice` — 学习行动
- `Don't be afraid of making mistakes.` — 鼓励表达

### Media and information（媒体素养）

- `be bombarded by pictures, articles, links and videos` — 信息轰炸
- `fake news` / `misinformation` — 虚假信息
- `check the source` / `watch out for fake photos` / `look for other signs` — 批判性检查步骤
- `don't get fooled` — 别被骗（口语化标题）
- `If the news story makes you angry, it's probably designed to make you angry.` — 扎心的真相

### Digital life and generations（数字习惯）

- `couldn't be more different` — 完全不同（强调结构）
- `missing out on spending time with friends in real life` — 错过真实社交
- `make contact with old friends` — 联系老朋友
- `It's changed my social life completely.` — 完全改变
- `setting a better example to my kids` — 树立好榜样
- `spending more quality time with them` — 高质量的陪伴时间
- `a less digital life` — 低数字化生活

### Career and professional（职业简介）

- `20 years' experience of designing and developing spaces` — 经验表达
- `skilled in negotiation, team motivation and building successful sales teams` — 技能列举
- `award-winning` — 获奖的
- `international experience of working in ...` — 国际经验
- `I am creative and hardworking, and enjoy working with others.` — 自我评价

### Literary and cultural（文学 + 文化）

- `squinted against the sun` — 眯眼看太阳（描写）
- `the more he kept out of his boss's way, the longer he'd have a job` — 因果关系
- `licking his lips and clearing his throat` — 紧张的身体语言
- `on the edge of your seat` — 紧张刺激（书评成语）
- `the reader can identify with the emotions` — 读者共情
- `We owe many of our modern ideas about fairies to Shakespeare.` — 归因表达
- `the earliest ... can be found in ...` — 起源追溯

## Grammar points worth noticing

### 短篇故事中的描写时态

- `Sam squinted against the sun at the distant dust trail raked up by the car.` — 过去时叙述 + 过去分词后置定语
- `His skin was already turning ruddy in the June sun.` — 过去进行时描写渐变状态
- `Carter twisted his hat round and round, licking his lips and clearing his throat.` — 并列动作 + 分词伴随状语

### 比较结构（Choosing a conference venue / English course prospectus）

- `Whether you are looking for X or Y, Z is the perfect ...` — 无论你需要 X 还是 Y
- `Conveniently located in ..., X is close to ... and only a ten-minute walk to ...`

### 条件 + 建议结构

- `If you have any questions or if you wish to reschedule, please call me.` — 正式邮件中的条件句
- `If you know these things ... then you have the control over what to read.` — 条件 = 行动的因果关系

## Recommended study order

按从易到难排列，适合 30 分钟日常阅读：

```
A2 阶段（可以马上用）：
1. Study skills tips           ← 最简单，祈使句 + 学习词汇
2. An end of term report       ← 表格阅读 + 简单评语
3. An email request for help   ← 短职场邮件
4. An invitation to a job interview ← 正式邮件格式

A2-B1 阶段（稍微复杂）：
5. English course prospectus   ← 两个选项比较、提取关键信息
6. Professional profile summaries ← 短但正式，CV 语言

B1 阶段（需要更多词汇）：
7. Choosing a conference venue ← 比较结构 + 商业描述
8. How to spot fake news       ← 指导性，4 个步骤结构清晰
9. Digital habits across generations ← 社会话题 + 数据引用
10. The legend of fairies       ← 文化说明 + 起源推理
11. The Martian a book review   ← 书评结构 + 评价表达
12. A short story extract       ← 文学描写，最难（含蓄的情感和氛围）
```

## Recommended study action

1. 优先使用 A2 的 4 篇（#1-#4）作为近期 daily reading 输入——每篇只需 5-8 分钟阅读 + 5 个 chunks + 2 句模仿。
2. "Study skills tips" 对学习者有直接实用价值——建议先读这篇，顺便当作学习建议。
3. B1 的 7 篇（#5-#12）按需选用，不要求全部掌握词汇。
4. "A short story extract" 是文学描写，适合将来做"感受氛围 > 理解每个词"的泛读训练。
5. "Professional profile summaries" 和 "An invitation to a job interview" 可用于未来 B1-B2 写作模仿。
6. "How to spot fake news" 和 "Digital habits across generations" 是很好的"事实 vs 观点"辨别材料。

## Mini output tasks

- **Study skills**: Write 4 sentences about your own English study habits. Use `I always ...`, `I sometimes ...`, `I try to ...`, and `I don't ...`.
- **Email request**: Write a short email asking a colleague for help on a project. Use `I would appreciate ...` and `It shouldn't take longer than ...`.
- **Job interview**: Write a 3-sentence reply to Anna Green confirming you will attend the interview.
- **Fake news**: List 3 things you can do to check if a story is real. Use short imperative sentences (`Check ...`, `Look for ...`, `Ask ...`).
- **Book review**: Write 3 sentences about a book or film you like. Use `It tells the story of ...`, `The main character is ...`, and `I recommend ...`.
