# Oxford Grammar Sources: Core A1-B2 Bundle

## Source

- Source files: `raw/sources/grammar/`
- Source type: A-level grammar reference / learner grammar explanations
- Approximate level: A1-B2

## Covered topics

### A1-A2 foundation

- Demonstratives: `this/that/these/those`
- Possessives: `my/your/his/her/its/our/their`, possessive pronouns, possessive `'s`
- Quantifiers: `some/any`, `much/many`, `a few/a little`
- Present perfect simple form and basic experience use

### B1 bridge

- Present perfect vs past simple
- Present perfect with `for/since`
- `just/already/yet`, `still/yet`
- Past perfect simple vs past simple
- Quantifiers: `both/several/most/all`, `each/every`, `more/less/fewer`, `too much/many`, `(not) enough`

### B2 extension

- Present perfect continuous vs present perfect simple
- Past perfect continuous form and contrast with past perfect simple

## High-value points for this learner

1. 先补 A1-A2 的所有格、指示词和数量词，因为它们会影响日常写作准确性。
2. Present perfect 是 B1 关键桥梁：重点掌握“经验/结果/持续到现在”和 past simple 的区别。
3. Past perfect 用于阅读理解和讲故事；不必过早追求复杂输出。
4. B2 阶段再系统比较 perfect simple vs perfect continuous，避免现在就过度学习细节。

## Recommended study action

每次只学一个小点，用 5-8 个自己的句子练习。优先顺序：

1. possessive adjectives + `it's` vs `its`
2. `some/any`, `much/many`, `a few/a little`
3. present perfect simple: `have/has + past participle`
4. present perfect vs past simple
5. `for/since`, `just/already/yet`
6. past perfect simple
7. perfect continuous contrasts
