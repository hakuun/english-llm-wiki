## Why it is useful

- 话题涉及食品安全、气候变化、供应链——是 B2 目标中"理解抽象/复杂话题"的典型材料。
- 包含英国数据（UK imports 40% of food），可以练习"听懂数字和事实"。
- 引入了 B2 级抽象词汇：`food security`（粮食安全）、`biodiversity loss`（生物多样性丧失）、`worst-case scenario`（最坏情况）。
- 展示了论证结构：问题（food supply is fragile）→ 原因（conflicts, climate change, biodiversity loss）→ 解决方案。

## Key vocabulary

| English | 中文 | Example from transcript |
|---------|------|------------------------|
| **food security** | 粮食安全 | "the term for having a reliable enough supply of affordable, healthy food to feed everyone" |
| **worst-case scenario** | 最坏情况 | "the worst possible thing that could happen in a particular situation" |
| **stark** | 明显的，严峻的 | "Its conclusions are really quite stark." |
| **livestock** | 家畜，牲畜 | "animals and birds kept on a farm, such as cows, sheep or chicken" |
| **piece of the puzzle** | 拼图的一部分（解决方案的一部分） | "part of the solution to a question or problem" |
| **part of the solution, not the problem** | 是解决方案的一部分，不是问题的一部分 | "if you are not actively trying to fix a problem, you are helping it continue" |

## Key phrases and collocations

- `the supply of fresh food is fragile` — 新鲜食物供应很脆弱
- `the supply chain can easily be disrupted` — 供应链容易被中断
- `carbon emissions from food production` — 食品生产的碳排放
- `geopolitical conflicts` — 地缘政治冲突
- `droughts and water shortages` — 干旱和水资源短缺
- `loss of biodiversity` — 生物多样性的丧失

## Comprehension focus

1. How much of its food does the UK import? (around 40%)
2. Which country does the UK import the most food from? (the Netherlands)
3. What are the main threats to UK food security? (geopolitical conflicts, droughts, water shortages, biodiversity loss, climate change)
4. What fraction of greenhouse gases comes from food production? (around one third)

## Shadowing recommendation

最佳跟读段落：介绍中描述食物供应脆弱性的一段（lines 72-73，从"The reality is that the supply..."到"climate change"），因为包含丰富的因果链和正式词汇。

## Retelling prompt

1. 英国的粮食安全面临哪些威胁？
2. "worst-case scenario" 是什么意思？举一个 food security 的 worst-case scenario。
3. 你觉得气候变化会怎么影响食物供应？（可以用中文说）

## Recommended study action

- 建议作为第四或第五篇听力材料（话题较抽象，涉及经济和政策）。
- 2 天循环。
- 学完后可以让学习者用 `piece of the puzzle` 和 `part of the solution, not the problem` 造句——这两个表达在 B2 论证中很有用。

## Difficulty note

这篇比 Animals 和 Cities 更抽象——涉及经济、政治、环境科学的交叉。如果学习者在听力理解上感到困难，可以先跳过，先完成前 3 篇更具体的主题。
