## Why it is useful

海滩/度假是日常闲聊高频话题，适合 A1-A2 阶段练习描述偏好和个人经历。对话中自然使用了现在完成时（`I've recently been to the beach`）和过去时（`I went to visit my parents`）的对比——可配合 present perfect 语法学习。pebble vs sandy beaches 的对比也是一个很好的实用描述模板。

## Key vocabulary

| Word | CEFR | Definition (from transcript) |

## Key chunks

- `I've recently been to the beach.` — 现在完成时描述近期经历
- `I went to visit my parents.` — 过去时描述具体动作（present perfect vs past simple 对比）
- `What have you been doing?` — 现在完成进行时问最近活动
- `I'll sunbathe for a little bit ... and then I'll go for a swim to cool down.` — `I'll + verb ... and then I'll + verb` 计划表达
- `Do you like to sunbathe ... or do you prefer to do something active?` — `like to A or prefer to B` 选择对比
- `I don't know about your own experiences, but ...` — 委婉表达不同意见
- `Have you got a memory of a favourite beach you've been to?` — 询问经历的好用句型

## Grammar points

- **现在完成时 vs 过去时**：Becca 说 `I've recently been to the beach`（经历）→ `I went to visit my parents`（具体事件）。这是 present perfect 的核心对比用法。
- **will + verb 做即时决定**：`I'll sunbathe ... and then I'll go for a swim` — 不是事先计划，而是对话中的即时描述。
- **比较表达**：sandy beaches are soft / pebble beaches can hurt your feet / it's easier to have a picnic on a pebble beach

## Shadowing 推荐段落

Becca 描述泰国海滩日落的段落（~15 秒，情感丰富，语调自然）：

> I just remember seeing the sunset at the beach, and I'd never seen the Sun so red before. It was incredible. So, when the Sun is setting and it looks like it's going below sea level, at night time. Just beautiful. Stunning.

## Retelling 提示

用 3-5 句英文回答：
- What's the difference between a sandy beach and a pebble beach?
- What does Becca like to do at the beach?
- Describe a beach you have been to (or would like to go to).
