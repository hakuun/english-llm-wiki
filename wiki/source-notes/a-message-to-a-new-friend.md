# A Message to a New Friend

## Source

- Source file: `raw/sources/reading/A message to a new friend.md`
- Source type: B-level graded reading / informal email
- Approximate level: A1-A2

## Why it is useful

这篇短邮件适合练习真实但简单的社交写作：问候、回忆见面、介绍地点、提出请求、结束邮件。

## Key vocabulary and chunks

- `It was so nice to meet you ...`
- `How was the rest of your trip?`
- `I hope you got home OK.`
- `We're going to be open in three months.`
- `The location is perfect.`
- `There are lots of restaurants ... in the area.`
- `Maybe you can come and see them one day?`
- `I would love to show you ...`
- `Please send me ...`
- `Bye for now`

## Useful sentence patterns

- `It was so nice to meet you last week in Sydney.`
- `I have the documents about the new Berlin offices.`
- `We're going to be open in three months.`
- `There are lots of restaurants, cafés and banks in the area.`
- `Please give me your email address and I will send you the documents.`

## Practice task

写一封 80-100 词的英文邮件给新朋友，必须包含：

1. `It was so nice to meet you ...`
2. 一个过去事件：`We met ... / That was ...`
3. 一个地点介绍：`There are ... in the area.`
4. 一个请求：`Please send me ...`
5. 一个结尾：`Bye for now`
