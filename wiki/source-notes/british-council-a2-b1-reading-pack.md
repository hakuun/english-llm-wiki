## Why it is useful

这组材料短、真实、任务明确，适合当前阶段做 30 分钟日常阅读输入。不要逐篇长篇总结；重点用来练习快速抓信息、提取常用表达、模仿简单邮件/通知/介绍类文本。

## Key chunks for active review

### Informal email and invitation

- `Just a quick email to say ...`
- `That sounds like a great idea.`
- `Saturday is better for me because ...`
- `Why don't you come here?`
- `It's going to be so good to catch up.`
- `Let me know if ...`
- `I'm really looking forward to seeing you.`

### Directions and travel rules

- `turn left at the post office`
- `keep going past the big white house`
- `call me if you get lost`
- `hand luggage`
- `no bigger than ...`
- `weigh no more than ...`
- `fit under the seat in front of you`
- `pay extra`
- `pass security`

### B1 work and service language

- `management styles`
- `give feedback`
- `deal with change`
- `in uncertain times`
- `international projects`
- `creative thinking`
- `conflict management`
- `on a month-to-month basis`
- `sign-up fees`
- `cancellation fees`
- `participate in regular social events`

### Travel guide language

- `on the way`
- `with plenty of things to see and do`
- `For something more authentic, ...`
- `get around the city`
- `stuck in traffic jams`
- `catch an express boat`
- `among the best in the world`
- `Head for Chinatown`
- `check out the snake farm`

## Sentence patterns worth imitating

- `Saturday is better for me because I'm meeting my parents on Sunday.`
- `If you turn left at the post office and keep going past the big white house, there's a small side street behind it.`
- `Passengers can only take one suitcase onto the plane.`
- `It must be no bigger than 55cm x 22cm x 35cm and weigh no more than 10kg.`
- `Consider how to ensure successful intercultural communication in your international teams.`
- `Membership works on a month-to-month basis.`
- `Bangkok might be noisy and polluted but it's also an exciting city with plenty of things to see and do.`
- `For something more authentic, Phra Kanong offers an alternative place to stay.`

## Grammar and reading points

- `if + present simple, will/can + verb`: `If you get lost, I'll come and get you.`
- rules and limits: `must`, `can only`, `cannot`, `will have to`
- comparison and alternatives: `better`, `more authentic`, `much better ways`
- service/process writing: `Step one`, `Step two`, `Finally`
- timetable scanning: time + title + purpose, not word-by-word translation

## Recommended study action

1. 近期每日学习优先选 A2 两篇：`An email from a friend` 和 `An airport notice`。
2. 每篇只做 5-8 个 chunks、3 个细节问题、3 句仿写。
3. B1 三篇先作为“扫读找信息”材料，不要求一次掌握全部词汇。
4. 可把 `A travel guide` 用作未来 B1 城市介绍写作模板。

## Mini output tasks

- Email: write 5 sentences inviting a friend to visit you. Use `Why don't you ...?` and `Let me know if ...`.
- Notice: write 4 simple rules for a train, library, or classroom. Use `must`, `can`, and `cannot`.
- Travel guide: write 5 sentences about your city. Use `There are ...`, `You can ...`, and `For something more ..., ...`.
