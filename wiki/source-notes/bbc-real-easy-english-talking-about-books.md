- Source type: B-level learning material / BBC Learning English podcast
- Approximate level: A2-B1（BBC 的 "Real Easy English" 系列故意使用更简单的词汇和较慢的语速）
- Duration: ~6 minutes
- Hosts: Neil and Becca

## Why it is useful

这是 5 份 BBC 听力材料中**最简单**的一篇，适合作为听力训练的第一篇。

- 话题贴近日常：讨论读书习惯和书籍类型——学习者已经熟悉的主题。
- 语速较慢，句子较短，A2 学习者也能跟上大意。
- 对话中有自然的重复和解释（如 "author — an author is a person who writes books"），降低理解负担。
- 末尾有词汇复述（recap）环节，非常适合跟读（shadowing）。

## Key vocabulary

| English | 中文 | Example from transcript |
|---------|------|------------------------|
| **fiction** | 小说（虚构类） | "Fiction is when the story is not true. It's made up by the writer." |
| **non-fiction** | 非虚构类 | "Have you ever read a non-fiction book?" |
| **novel** | 长篇小说 | "Novels are fiction books. That's just one quite long story." |
| **biography** | 传记 | "A biography is somebody's life story." |
| **autobiography** | 自传 | "If that person wrote the story of their own life, it's called an autobiography." |
| **poem** | 诗，诗歌 | "Poems are a shorter piece of writing... The sounds of the words and the ideas are very important." |

## Key phrases and collocations

- `How often do you read?` — 询问频率
- `What kind of books do you like to read?` — 询问偏好
- `I prefer...` — 表达偏好
- `I only read when I'm in a relaxed environment.` — 描述条件
- `some of my favourite authors` — 描述喜好

## Comprehension focus

1. What types of books does Becca like? (short books, poems)
2. What types of books does Neil like? (fiction, novels, biographies of musicians)
3. Where does Neil do his serious reading? (when travelling to and from work)
4. Why does Becca like poems? (they are short, emotional, have nice sound quality)

## Shadowing recommendation

最佳跟读段落：词汇复述部分（lines 105-117），因为：
- 语速较慢
- 是对话的总结，句子完整
- 每个词都带解释，容易跟上

## Retelling prompt

用中文引导学习者复述：

1. Neil 和 Becca 各自喜欢读什么类型的书？
2. 他们分别在什么时候/什么地方读书？
3. 你喜欢读什么类型的书？用英文说 3 句。

## Recommended study action

- 作为听力训练的第一篇（2 天循环）。
- Day 1：不看 transcript 听一遍 → 读 transcript → 再听一遍 → 学 5-6 个词汇
- Day 2：跟读词汇复述段 → 用中文引导做简短复述
- 难度低，成功后可以建立听力信心，再进入 B1-B2 的 6 Minute English。

## Difficulty note

这是最简单的一篇。如果连这篇都觉得难，说明听力需要从更基础的 A1-A2 材料开始（如 BBC Learning English 的 "Starting Out" 系列）。
