- Duration: ~6 minutes

## Why it is useful

- 话题与日常生活高度相关——借钱、还债、生活成本——学习者在中文中已经理解这些概念。
- 包含 3 个"还债"的同义表达（`pay back`, `pay off`, `clear debt`），展示了英语中同一概念可以有多种表达方式——这是 B2 词汇丰富度的关键。
- 引入了英国社会问题的真实数据（Citizens Advice Bureau 每天 1000+ 人求助债务问题），练习"理解数据"。
- 包含两个地道习语：`make ends meet`（收支平衡）和 `on the never-never`（分期付款）——在 B1-B2 听力中高频出现。
- 口音为英音（RP/Standard Southern British），清晰标准。

## Key vocabulary

| English | 中文 | Example from transcript |
|---------|------|------------------------|
| **debt** | 债务 | "money a person has borrowed and owes to someone" |
| **pay back / pay off / clear (debt)** | 还债 | "You can't ever pay it back." / "I'd like to think I could clear them all one day." |
| **make ends meet** | 收支平衡，勉强维生 | "how they were making ends meet — having just enough money to pay for basic living expenses" |
| **in arrears** | 欠款的，逾期的 | "being behind with one or more monthly payment commitments" |
| **the never-never** | 分期付款（非正式） | "buying something with one large payment followed by smaller regular payments" |
| **financial strain** | 经济压力 | "emotional stress caused by a lack of money to meet basic needs or repay debt" |

## Key phrases and collocations

- `borrow money` — 借钱
- `take out loans` — 申请贷款
- `get out of control` — 失控
- `count every penny` — 精打细算
- `a way out` — 出路
- `an ordinary feature of daily life` — 日常生活的普通部分

## Comprehension focus

1. What is the difference between "borrow" and "owe"? (borrow = take money, owe = have to give money back)
2. What is the average British adult's debt through credit cards, overdrafts and personal loans? (£4,200)
3. Why did some people in Woldham stop trying to pay their debts? (financial strain increased, they missed bills, internet disconnected — debt became an ordinary feature of daily life)
4. What does "on the never-never" mean and why is it called that? (buying by making many small payments over a long time — it seems the debt will never be repaid)

## Retelling prompt

1. 英国人平均欠多少信用卡和个人贷款债务？
2. "make ends meet" 是什么意思？用你自己的话解释。
3. Jason 的故事中，债务对生活有什么影响？
4. (Challenge) 你觉得为什么很多人不愿意谈论自己的债务问题？用简单英语说 2-3 句。

## Recommended study action

- 建议作为第四或第五篇听力材料（财务类词汇较集中，可能需要更多词汇预习）。
- 2 天循环。
- 重点练习 3 个"还债"同义表达——这是 B2 词汇多样性的好例子。

## Difficulty note

财务类词汇较集中（debt, loan, arrears, credit cards, overdrafts），如果学习者没有相关中文概念背景，可能需要额外 2-3 分钟词汇预习。建议放在"Future of food"之后或之前学习，两者都是较抽象的社会话题。
